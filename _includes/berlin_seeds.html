In the bustling metropolis of Berlin, homicide detective Olaf Bauer struggles with the aftermath of a shootout. An organ trafficking gang claimed his partner’s life, and Olaf holds himself responsible. So when a young woman is murdered with her ovaries missing, he can’t shake the feeling his partner died for nothing.


Olaf’s boss assigns him a new partner for the investigation: Julia Teichmann, a sharp-witted divorcee with a young daughter and a fiery temperament. As Olaf and Julia are adjusting to working together, a second murder occurs. The same gruesome signature is present, and the duo soon realizes they’re dealing with a serial killer.


With the clock ticking, Olaf and Julia will confront their own family histories to uncover the killer’s identity. But in the absence of clear motives and leads, the perpetrator could be anyone. Will they crack the case before the next strike?
