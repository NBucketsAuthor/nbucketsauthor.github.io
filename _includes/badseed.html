In Berlin, a young woman is found murdered and her ovaries are missing.


Homicides detective Olaf Bauer, who’s been struggling since an organ trafficking gang killed his partner during an undercover operation, is called on the case.


His boss teams him up with Julia Teichmann, a recent divorcée in turmoil with a young daughter and her own troubles, opening up Bauer’s emotional wound.


As Olaf and Julia lead the investigation and adjust to working together, a second woman is murdered exactly like the first victim, and the killer could be anybody—even Teichmann’s father.


Who is behind the serial gruesome murders?

And how is Olaf’s brother entangled in the case?
