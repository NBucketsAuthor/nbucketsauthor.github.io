It started years ago. It never really ended.

Book 2 in the Olaf Bauer series — can be read as a standalone.

Detective Olaf Bauer is pulled into a fresh murder case. A dead journalist. A missing politician. And Max, newly released on a legal loophole.

Four strangers. One buried secret. And a case that connects them all to the man Olaf should never have trusted.

What happened twenty years ago?
And who’s still willing to kill to keep it hidden?
